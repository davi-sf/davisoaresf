About Me

Hello! My name is Davi, I'm 23 years old and I'm from Cotia-SP. Currently, I'm a Computer Science student at Federal University of Campina Grande(UFCG) and I'm constantly seeking challenges that allow me to expand my knowledge and skills In software development, I have been exploring various languages, including Java, Python, and JavaScript. Additionally, I have knowledge of databases such as PostgreSQL, MySQL, and NoSQL. I have started to work on projects and studies applied to Spring Boot, as well as web development technologies With the aim of building a solid foundation and preparing myself to face the challenges of the software development field. At the age of 16, I had the great opportunity to live in Canada for six months, through a high school exchange program. During my stay there, I improved my English, a skill that I later put into practice as an English teacher. Additionally, I am also a volunteer music teacher, where I share my passion for music and my ability to play the guitar. Besides my dedication to studies, I seek to apply that dedication to practical experiences. I have worked as a call center attendant, where I applied my basic technical knowledge to develop two simple but useful applications using HTML5, CSS3 And JavaScript, both for me and my colleagues. These tools are still used in the operation today and are available in my portfolio, where I demonstrate my intention to apply technical concepts in practical situations. Despite my limited practical experience, I am always looking to learn something new and improve my existing skills. Lastly, when I'm not programming, I'm playing the guitar, a hobby that inspires me and allows me to live music, which is a passion already mentioned above.
