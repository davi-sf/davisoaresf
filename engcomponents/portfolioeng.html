Protocol Records Automator

I developed a project to automate the registration and completion of calls in the call center, which consists of linking each call to its respective protocol. This initiative aims to simplify and accurately record all the details of the interactions, eliminating the need to manually perform this task at the end of each call. Thus, the project allows for more efficient and organized management of customer service, providing a better experience for customers and optimizing the team's workflow. Today this tool has been adopted in the environment by several people.

Energy Consumption Calculator

I developed a specialized repository to calculate the final consumption of electricity, simplifying and streamlining the process that was previously prone to errors and time-consuming. This tool was especially useful in the dynamic environment of a call center, where agents can enter the necessary data and get the calculation quickly and accurately when finishing the calls. The success of the project is evident, with many colleagues using this tool as an essential part of their daily customer service operations.
