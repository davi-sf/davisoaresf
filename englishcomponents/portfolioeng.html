Protocol Records Automator

I developed a project to automate call registration and completion in the call center, which involves linking each call to its respective protocol. This initiative aims to simplify and accurately record all interaction details, eliminating the need to manually enter everything that happened in the call at the end of each conversation. Thus, the project enables more efficient and organized management of customer service, providing a better experience for clients and optimizing team workflow. Today, this tool has been adopted by several individuals in the company.

Energy Consumption Calculator

I developed a specialized repository to calculate the final consumption of electricity, simplifying and streamlining the process that was previously prone to errors and time-consuming. This tool was especially useful in the dynamic environment of a call center, where agents can enter the necessary data and get the calculation quickly and accurately, when the clients wanted to call of the contract The success of the project is evident, with many colleagues using this tool as an essential part of their daily customer service operations.
